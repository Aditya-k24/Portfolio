# 🌟 Janani: An E-Learning Platform for Underprivileged Women

During my **first year of engineering**, my team and I participated in a **national-level hackathon**, and it was an unforgettable journey! We developed **Janani**, an **e-learning platform** designed to empower **underprivileged women** by providing them with access to acclaimed courses, enabling them to gain valuable skills, **become financially independent**, and achieve self-reliance.  

I’m thrilled to share that **Janani** earned us a **finalist spot** at the hackathon, competing against some of the most talented teams nationwide.

## 🎯 **What is Janani?**

**Janani** is a platform dedicated to bridging the education gap for women who lack access to quality resources. It is an **all-in-one e-learning solution** designed to:  
- **Provide access to industry-acclaimed courses** in areas such as technology, business, and arts.  
- **Help users gain employable skills** to kickstart their careers or launch small businesses.  
- Foster a supportive and empowering environment for learning.  

Our platform is built with **user-friendly features** tailored for women from diverse backgrounds, ensuring inclusivity and accessibility.  

---

## 🔧 **Technology Stack**

To build Janani, we leveraged the power of modern web development technologies:  

- **MERN Stack**:  
  - **MongoDB**: For storing course data, user profiles, and progress.  
  - **Express.js**: To build a scalable and secure backend API.  
  - **React.js**: For crafting an interactive and intuitive user interface.  
  - **Node.js**: To handle server-side logic and integrations.  

- **Authentication & Security**:  
  - Implemented secure user login and profile management using **JWT (JSON Web Tokens)**.  

- **Responsive Design**:  
  - Ensured the platform is accessible across devices using **Bootstrap** and **CSS Flexbox/Grid**.  

- **Payment Gateway Integration**:  
  - Incorporated payment systems to handle **donations** and affordable course subscriptions.  

---

## 🌟 **Key Features of Janani**

1. **Curated Courses**:  
   A wide range of affordable or free courses designed to provide real-world skills.  

2. **Progress Tracking**:  
   Visual dashboards to track learning progress and milestones.  

3. **Job Resources**:  
   Integration with job boards and freelance platforms to connect users with opportunities.  

4. **Community Support**:  
   Discussion forums and mentorship opportunities to foster a supportive learning environment.  

5. **Multi-Lingual Support**:  
   Courses available in regional languages to improve accessibility.
